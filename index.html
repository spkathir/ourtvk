About Our Village

Thiruvarangulam is a vibrant village located in the Alangudi taluka of Pudukkottai district, Tamil Nadu. With a rich history spanning over 2000 years, our village is home to the ancient Arangulanathar Temple built by the great Karikala Chola.

PIN Code: 622303
Distance from District HQ: 6 km
Elevation: 90 meters
Taluka: Alangudi
